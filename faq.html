Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Scouts Emergency Response?

Scouts Emergency Response (SER) is a global initiative that trains scouts to respond effectively to emergencies and disasters.

How can I join Scouts Emergency Response?

You can join by registering through our official website or contacting your local scout group.

What training do members receive?

Members receive training in first aid, disaster response, and emergency preparedness.

Are there any age restrictions to join?

No, SER welcomes individuals of all ages who are interested in making a difference in their communities.
